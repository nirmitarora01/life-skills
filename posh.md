# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

* Making unwanted sexual comments or jokes
* Unwanted physical contact or touching
* Sending inappropriate messages or images
* Staring, leering, or intrusive gestures
* Any behaviour that creates a hostile or uncomfortable environment

These behaviours make people feel unsafe, disrespected, or intimidated at the workplace.

## 2. What would you do if you face or witness any incident of such behaviour?

If I face or witness harassment, I would:

* Clearly tell the person to stop if I feel safe doing so
* Document the date, time, and details of the incident
* Report the behaviour to HR or the PoSH committee immediately
* Share evidence like messages or witnesses if available
* Ask for support from a trusted colleague or supervisor
