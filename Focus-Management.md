# Focus Management

### Question 1  
What is Deep Work?

Deep Work means working with full concentration on one difficult task without any distractions so that you produce high quality results.

### Question 2  
According to the author, how to do deep work properly?

* Plan your deep work time in advance  
* Remove distractions like phone and social media  
* Work in fixed time blocks  
* Train your mind to focus longer  
* Protect your focused hours seriously  

### Question 3  
How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

* Keep my phone away while coding   
* Avoid multitasking  
* Take short breaks after long focus sessions  
* Say no to unnecessary interruptions  

### Question 4  
What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

* It reduces attention span  
* It wastes time without us realizing  
* It interrupts deep thinking  
* It affects sleep and mental peace
