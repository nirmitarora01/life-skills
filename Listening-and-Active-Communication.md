# Listening and Active Communication

## 1. Active Listening

### Question 1  
What are the steps or strategies to do Active Listening?

* Pay full attention to the speaker
* Avoid interrupting while the other person is speaking
* Maintain eye contact and positive body language
* Listen to understand, not to reply
* Ask relevant clarifying questions
* Summarize what the speaker has said
* Acknowledge the speaker’s feelings.

---

## 2. Reflective Listening

### Question 2  
According to Fisher’s model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?

* Carefully listen to the speaker’s message
* Observe emotions and body language
* Paraphrase the message in simple words
* Verify understanding with the speaker
* Correct misunderstandings immediately
* Ensure both sides agree on the same meaning

---

## 3. Reflection

### Question 3  
What are the obstacles in your listening process?

* Getting distracted by mobile or surroundings
* Thinking about my response while the other person is talking
* Assumptions based on past experiences
* Lack of interest in the topic

### Question 4  
What can you do to improve your listening?

* Focus completely on the speaker
* Take short notes during discussions
* Avoid multitasking during conversations
* Ask questions to confirm understanding
* Practice patience and empathy

---

## 4. Types of Communication

### Question 5  
When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?

* When talking to seniors or authority figures
* When I want to avoid conflict
* When I feel unsure about my opinion

### Question 6  
When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

* When I feel frustrated 
* When deadlines are tight

### Question 7  
When do you switch into Passive Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

* When I hesitate to speak directly
* When I feel uncomfortable expressing disagreement
* During emotional or stressful situations

### Question 8  
How can you make your communication assertive?

* Clearly express my needs and expectations
* Use calm and respectful language
* Listen actively before responding
* Maintain confident body language
* Give and receive feedback constructively
