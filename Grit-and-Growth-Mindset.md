# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit

### Answer 1  

The video explains that grit is the ability to stay committed to long term goals with passion and perseverance, even when progress is slow or failures occur.

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

### Answer 2  

The video explains that a growth mindset is the belief that skills and intelligence can be improved through effort, learning, and persistence rather than being fixed.

---

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

### Answer 3  

Internal locus of control means believing that your actions and decisions control your results. The key point of the video is that taking responsibility for outcomes helps build motivation and long term success.

---

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

### Answer 4  

* Accept challenges instead of avoiding them  
* Learn from mistakes and failures  
* Put consistent effort into learning  
* Stay curious and keep improving  
* Seek feedback and act on it  

---

## 5. Reflection on Growth Mindset as a Software Developer

### Answer 5  

* Treat mistakes as learning opportunities  
* Practice regularly and improve step by step  
* Ask questions without fear  
* Take ownership of tasks and outcomes  
* Stay patient during difficult problems
